About

We are excited to announce this conference jointly organised by University College London (UK) and Peking University (China).

The hippocampal formation plays a critical role in cognitive processes such as spatial navigation and emotion. A number of neurological disorders, including dementia, depression, schizophrenia etc, are associated with hippocampal dysfunction. Through a series of talks, posters and discussion sessions, this two day symposium will bring together experimenters and theoreticians to discuss the latest developments in the field, focusing on both the physiological and pathological aspects.
