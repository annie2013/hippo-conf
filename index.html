About

We are excited to announce this conference jointly organised by University College London (UCL, UK) and Peking University (PKU, China).

The hippocampal formation plays a critical role in cognitive processes such as spatial navigation and emotion. It is also associated with a number of neurological disorders, including dementia, depression, and schizophrenia.

Through a series of talks, posters and discussion sessions, this two day conference will bring together experimenters and theoreticians to discuss the latest developments in the field, focusing on both the physiological and pathological aspects of this fascinating reigon of the brain.
